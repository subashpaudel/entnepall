Ear Surgery




Ear surgery is the treatment of diseases, injuries, or deformations of the ear by operation with instruments. In some cases surgery is the only treatment to treat diseases, injuries or deformities of the ear's auditory tube, middle ear, inner ear, and auditory and vestibular systems.
